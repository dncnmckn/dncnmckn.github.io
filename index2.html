Bio

Duncan McKean is a human-centred designer who works with organisations on projects that impact society. Several of his projects have been recognised in national competitions, and his work has appeared on high-profile design industry websites. He has also held courses in Rhino 3D, creative methodologies and taught university students the digital design skills they need to be competitive designers. // designing for good // open // collaborative

When he moved to the Netherlands in 2009 to be with his family, he set himself up as an Independent Designer. His holistic approach to design, creativity and strategy has helped him gain amazing clients such as the Global Reporting Initiative, David Lynch Foundation, Greenpeace, ING, Elsevier and Deltafix.

He also currently runs a small (yet perfectly formed) creative studio just outside of Amsterdam that makes fun stuff.

He likes his tea hot, with no sugar.
